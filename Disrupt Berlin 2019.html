Frequently Asked Questions

Contact TechCrunch at events@techcrunch.com if you have any additional questions.

What is the refund / transfer policy for Disrupt Berlin passes?

All purchases are NON-REFUNDABLE but you can transfer your pass to another person up until 8 December before the event by following these instructions.

Will you be serving food at Disrupt Berlin?

There will be several food truck vendors located at the Arena Berlin with food and drinks available for purchase. Make sure to bring cash!

Do you charge VAT on pass purchases?

Yes, VAT is a sales tax which is levied at all levels of taxable supplies of goods and services made by a taxable person in the course of any business activity carried on in Germany. The VAT rate in Germany is 19% and will be added to all pass purchases for Disrupt Berlin. If your country provides a refund on German VAT, you will need to apply for that refund directly through them.

Can I pay for my passes via wire or check?

Please contact events@techcrunch.com to request an invoice be generated for you to remit upon. Please make sure to provide the appropriate business address, type and number of passes that you would like to request.
